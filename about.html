About Us

Built on Trust. Driven by Change.

Delta Companies: Construction is a locally owned construction and renovation company serving homeowners and businesses throughout the Memphis area. Our focus is simple: deliver high-quality work, communicate clearly, and treat every project like it's our own property.

We specialize in residential renovations, remodeling, and construction projects that improve how people live in their homes. Whether it's updating a kitchen, transforming a bathroom, finishing a space, or completing a larger renovation, our team works closely with clients to turn their ideas into well-built, lasting results.

At Delta Companies: Construction, we believe construction projects should be organized, transparent, and predictable. Too often homeowners face unclear pricing, poor communication, or projects that take longer than expected. Our process focuses on clear estimates, thoughtful planning, and consistent project management so clients always know what to expect.

We are proud to serve the Memphis community and take seriously the trust clients place in us when inviting us into their homes. Delta Companies: Construction is committed to building long-term relationships through reliable work, honest communication, and craftsmanship that stands the test of time.

Our Process

How We Work

1

Initial Consultation & Estimate

We start with a conversation about your project goals, timeline, and budget. Our team visits the site, evaluates the scope of work, and provides a clear, detailed estimate so you know exactly what to expect before work begins.

2

Design & Project Planning

Once approved, we finalize the design, materials, and construction plan. We coordinate any necessary permits and scheduling so the project moves forward smoothly and efficiently.

3

Construction & Project Management

Our experienced team completes the work with a focus on quality craftsmanship and attention to detail. Throughout the build, we maintain clear communication and keep the project on schedule.

4

Final Walk-Through & Handover

When construction is finished, we walk through the project with you to ensure everything meets expectations. We address any final details and make sure you're completely satisfied with the finished result.
